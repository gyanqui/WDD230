Get to know us!

We're a small Carlsbad-based business offering organic, locally grown food. At Bountiful Foods, we serve our customers through our storefront and food trucks along the coastal area from LA to San Diego. Our mission is to provide fresh, healthy options like made-to-order fruit drinks and bags. We source from local farmers and suppliers for quality products.
