Who are we?

Bountiful Foods is a thriving small business based in Carlsbad, California, dedicated to bringing the goodness of organic, locally grown foods to the community and beyond. With a passion for promoting health and sustainability, they source the freshest produce directly from local farmers, while also collaborating with national and international suppliers to offer a diverse range of high-quality options.

As a trailblazer in the healthy eating landscape, Bountiful Foods caters to a wide audience through their charming storefront in Carlsbad and a fleet of food trucks that cover the picturesque coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego. Their mission is to provide convenient, made-to-order fruit drinks and delightful fruit bags, encouraging a wholesome lifestyle among their valued customers.

Rooted in their owner-operated spirit, Bountiful Foods takes pride in creating a positive impact on the environment and supporting the local community. Their warm and inviting storefront, coupled with the seamless online and phone ordering system, ensures that everyone can enjoy their organic bounty.
