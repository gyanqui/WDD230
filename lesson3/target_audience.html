Purpose Target Audience Design

Target Audience

  • Tourists planning a visit to Cusco and surrounding areas
  • Local businesses and entrepreneurs
  • Investors looking for business opportunities in Cusco
  • Residents of Cusco and the surrounding region

Personas

Persona 1: Adventure Seeker

Name: Juan Rodriguez

Age: 30

Occupation: Software Engineer

Background: Juan loves adventure and exploring new places. He's an experienced traveler and is always looking for his next adventure. He's heard a lot about Cusco and Machu Picchu and wants to plan a trip there.

Persona 2: Local Business Owner

Name: Maria Gomez

Age: 45

Occupation: Restaurant Owner

Background: Maria owns a small restaurant in Cusco and is looking to grow her business. She's interested in learning more about the resources available to small business owners in the area and is also looking for ways to attract more tourists to her restaurant.

Scenarios

  • Adventure Seeker: Juan is researching his upcoming trip to Cusco and wants to find information about the best hiking trails in the area.
  • Local Business Owner: Maria is looking for information about local business networking events.
  • Tourist: Sarah is planning her first trip to Cusco and wants to learn more about the history and culture of the area.
  • Investor: John is considering investing in a local business in Cusco and wants to find out more about the economic climate in the region.
  • Resident: Pedro is interested in attending cultural events in Cusco and wants to find out what's happening in the area this weekend.
