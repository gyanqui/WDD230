Non-Profit:

  • Access to exclusive newsletters and updates.
  • Invitations to attend community events and workshops.
  • Cost: $0 (No membership fee for non-profit organizations)

Bronze:

  • Access to exclusive newsletters and updates.
  • Invitations to attend community events and workshops.
  • Priority registration for events and workshops.
  • Enhanced recognition as a Bronze-level supporter.
  • Cost: $49.99

Silver:

  • Access to exclusive newsletters and updates.
  • Invitations to attend community events and workshops.
  • Priority registration for events and workshops.
  • Discounted rates for attending conferences and seminars.
  • Business information prominently displayed on the Chamber homepage.
  • Recognition as a Silver-level supporter with a personalized certificate.
  • Cost: $69.99

Gold:

  • Access to exclusive newsletters and updates.
  • Invitations to attend community events and workshops.
  • Priority registration for events and workshops.
  • Discounted rates for attending conferences and seminars.
  • Business information prominently displayed on the Chamber homepage.
  • Recognition as a Gold-level supporter on the organization's website and promotional materials.
  • Personalized mentorship opportunities with industry experts.
  • VIP access to exclusive events and private receptions.
  • Special acknowledgment during major events and conferences.
  • Cost: $99.99
