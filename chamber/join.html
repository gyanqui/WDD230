Membership Level
Levels

See level benefits below:

Non-profit

Directory listing on the Chamber website, showcasing your non-profit organization's mission and activities. Increased visibility within the Cusco community, reaching potential supporters and collaborators. Access to networking opportunities with local non-profit leaders and government officials. Special member pricing/discounts for non-profit workshops, seminars, and fundraising events. Opportunity to participate in Chamber committees focused on non-profit initiatives. Cost: $0 (No membership fee for non-profit organizations)

Bronze

1 reserved seat at Monthly Chamber Luncheons featuring influential business and government leaders in Cusco. Learn from top industry experts about business strategies, sustainable tourism, and community development. Monthly Foursomes with Carts and Range Balls at prestigious golf courses in Cusco, fostering networking among business professionals. Logo prominently displayed on the Chamber's website directory page, increasing visibility among potential partners. Company name listed in the footer of the weekly Chamber newsletter email, reaching a broad audience. Cost: $20

Silver

Sponsorship credit of $500 on select events, showcasing your company's commitment to the economic growth of Cusco. Logo visibility on all digital and printed materials, strengthening brand recognition. Banner recognition with logo displayed at Chamber events, including trade shows, conferences, and business expos. 4 reserved seats at the Annual Cusco Business Summit, providing networking opportunities with key industry leaders. 2 reserved seats at Monthly Chamber Luncheons, gaining insights from influential business professionals and government officials. Cost: $35

Gold

Sponsorship credit of $1,500 on select events, positioning your company as a prominent supporter of the Cusco business community. Exclusive invitation to participate in the Executive Round Table, engaging in high-level discussions and decision-making processes. 2 reserved seats at Monthly Chamber Luncheons, connecting with top business leaders and government officials. Logo prominently displayed on all digital and printed materials, maximizing brand exposure. Banner recognition with logo prominently displayed at Chamber events, emphasizing your company's leadership and commitment. 6 reserved seats at the Annual Cusco Business Summit, fostering partnerships with influential professionals from multiple sectors. Weekly Foursomes with Carts at renowned golf clubs in Cusco, creating networking opportunities in a relaxed setting. Cost: $50
